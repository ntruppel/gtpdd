Scorigami is a concept originally thought up by Jon Bois and applied to NFL games. The idea is to show every combination of football scores ever accomplished. His version showed every outcome of every NFL game, while ours shows Louisiana Tech games.

Hover over a square (or long press if using a touchscreen) to view the last time(s) Tech had a game that ended with one of these scores.
